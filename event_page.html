KOREAN EVENT AND FESTIVALS

Korea, being a country of four distinct seasons (spring, summer, autumn & winter), well-known music scene and strong cultural background held many interesting festivals throughout the year.
Seoul is the best place to see the beauty of Korea.
Seoul is home to many festivals, pageants and events throughout the year.
Some of the most significant festivals in Seoul are:

Lotus Lantern Festival

The Lotus Lantern Festival (Yeon Deung Hoe) is held annually on the eighth day of the fourth lunar month to celebrate Buddha's birthday. Thousands of handmade lanterns are displayed on the streets. There are various events, such as lantern parades, lotus-lantern making demonstrations, street events and dancing. Festival events are free to all and attract thousands of spectators..

Seoul Friendship Fair

This is a festival of free events, concerts, exhibitions and performances organised by the Seoul city government. The festival features traditional music and dance groups representing cultures from around the world, as well as local artists and vendors from Seoul's varied communities. The festival takes place in May every year.

Hi Seoul Festival

The Hi Seoul Festival is the biggest festival in Seoul and is a leading arts event taking place in May. There are fireworks, dance contests, cultural events and concerts. Festival events take place in different parts of the city.
