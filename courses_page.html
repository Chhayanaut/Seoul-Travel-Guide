Buisness visitor class

The Gangbuk and Gangnam courses are for business travelers who are busy on business trips in Korea.

(Gangbuk) Gyeongbokgung Palace -> Gwanghwamun Square -> Cheonggyecheon -> Tium SK Telecom ICT Exhibition Hall
(Gangnam) Bongeunsa Temple -> PC room and VR game

Gyeongbokgung Palace

Korea's representative palace where you can feel the history and dignity of the Joseon Dynasty. You can experience looking around Gyeongbokgung Palace in hanbok

Gwanghwamun Square

Gwanghwamun, the gateway to Gyeong bok gung Palace, the representative palace of Korea and Seoul. The square, which runs from Gwang hwamun Gate, is a cultural/rest area for citizens.

Cheonggyecheon

It is loved by Seoul citizens as it has a walking trail along the waterway from Cheonggye Plaza, close to the Sejong Center, to Dongdaemun Design Plaza & Park (DDP).

Tium SK Telecom ICT Exhibition Hall

The exhibition hall provides a program to experience the future of life using advanced ICT technology that comes to mind when you think of Seoul. You can use it by visiting through pre-booking.

Bongeunsa Temple

It is one of Seoul's representative temples and is located in the middle of the downtown of Samseong-dong, making it a symbolic space in Seoul where the past and modern coexist. It also runs a temple stay program.

PC room and VR game

If you pay the hourly fee, you can enjoy unique entertainment in Seoul, from PC games to various experiences using virtual reality devices.
