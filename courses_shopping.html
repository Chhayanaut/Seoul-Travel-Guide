Seoul Shopping Course

Shopping courses for tourists coming to Seoul include Myeong-dong, Insa-dong, and Gwangjang Market, which are hot places.

Myeongdong -> Insadong -> Gwangjang Market

Myeongdong

The largest shopping area in central Seoul, with department stores, duty-free shops, various global brands and street food.

Insadong

It is a place where you can see the most Korean products with craftsmen's shops, traditional galleries, and galleries. A representative place for foreign tourists to buy Korean souvenirs.

Gwangjang Market

It is a traditional market full of basic household items such as hanbok and bedding, and is also famous for various market restaurants such as bindaetteok and drug kimbap.
